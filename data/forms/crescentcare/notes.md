# CrescentCare — ROI Form Retrieval Notes

## Organization
- **Name:** CrescentCare
- **Website:** https://www.crescentcare.org/
- **Location:** New Orleans, LA
- **Type:** FQHC, independent, nonprofit

## Search Strategies

1. **Web search for PDF:** Searched `CrescentCare New Orleans "authorization" "release" "health information" filetype:pdf` — did not directly return results.
2. **Web search for release form:** Searched `CrescentCare New Orleans "medical records" "release form" filetype:pdf` — returned the direct PDF URL for the English ROI form.
3. **Site-specific search:** Searched `site:crescentcare.org authorization release medical records` — confirmed the form URL and also found the records request page and patient resources page.
4. **Patient Resources page:** Navigated to https://www.crescentcare.org/patient-resources-and-downloads/ — found the ROI form listed in both English and Spanish, along with many other patient forms.

## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.crescentcare.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Release-of-CrescentCare-Medical-Information._en.pdf
- **Document type:** ROI / Authorization for Release of Medical Information (English)
- **Description:** Single-page authorization form for release of CrescentCare protected health information. Well-structured with sections for patient info, recipient info, information to be disclosed (checkboxes for complete record, immunization, lab results, visit notes, dental, diagnostic reports), special authorization for sensitive records (behavioral health, substance use, HIV/AIDS), purpose of disclosure, expiration (defaults to 1 year), patient rights acknowledgments, and signature blocks. Offers delivery via patient portal, encrypted email, or pickup.

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.crescentcare.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Release-of-CrescentCare-Medical-Information_es.pdf
- **Document type:** ROI / Authorization for Release of Medical Information (Spanish)
- **Description:** Spanish translation of form-1.pdf. Identical structure and content ("Autorización Para la Divulgación de Información Médica de CrescentCare").

## Difficulties
- None. The form was easy to find via web search and prominently linked from the Patient Resources and Downloads page. CrescentCare also has a dedicated "Request your medical records" page at https://www.crescentcare.org/records-request-form/ with an online submission option.
