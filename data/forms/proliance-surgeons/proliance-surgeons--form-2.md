**Authorization vs. Right of Access conflation**: The form is structured as an authorization under ¬ß 164.508, not a Right of Access request under ¬ß 164.524. While providers often use authorization forms for patient access requests, this conflation means patients requesting their own records are subject to authorization-specific requirements (e.g., specifying a recipient, stating a reason) that HIPAA does not require for access requests.
