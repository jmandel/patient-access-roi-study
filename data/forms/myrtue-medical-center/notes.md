## Forms Found

The organization has a dedicated "Authorization + Release Forms" page under Patients + Visitors, listing 4 forms (2 in English, 2 Spanish translations):

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://www.myrtuemedical.org/documents/authorization%20&%20release%20forms/ROI-English-06-17-25.pdf
- **Document type**: Authorization to Release Protected Health Information (ROI form)
- **Description**: This is the primary ROI form. It is a 1-page HIPAA Privacy Form that allows patients to authorize release of medical records. Includes fields for patient info, from/to addresses (with checkboxes for Myrtue locations including Behavioral Health and Community Health), date range, record types (entire record, mental/behavioral health, radiology, lab/pathology, substance use disorder), purpose of release, and delivery method options (MyChart Portal, mail, encrypted email, in-person pickup). Includes legal disclosures about reproduction fees, right to revoke, expiration (max 1 year), and re-disclosure warnings.
- **File size**: 490,858 bytes
- **Pages**: 1

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://www.myrtuemedical.org/documents/authorization%20&%20release%20forms/Consent-for-Disclosure-English-07-28-25[1].pdf
- **Document type**: Consent for Disclosure — Permission to Leave Phone Messages and/or Verbally Discuss Protected Health Information
- **Description**: A separate 1-page form that authorizes Myrtue Medical Center to verbally share medical information with specified individuals (family, caregivers) involved in patient care or payment. Includes fields for up to 4 authorized individuals with name, relationship, and phone. Has checkboxes for substance use/abuse and mental health information. Valid for 1 year from signing.
