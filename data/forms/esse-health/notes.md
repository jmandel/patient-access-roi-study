## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.essehealth.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/Authorization-for-Release-of-Medical-Information-%E2%80%93-For-Records-Transfer-or-Release.pdf
- **Document type:** Authorization for Release of Medical Information (ROI form)
- **Description:** Standard medical records release/transfer authorization form. Includes patient name, DOB, from/to fields, checkboxes for record types (complete record, allergies, x-rays, progress notes, problem list, EKG, immunizations, lab reports, medications, consultations, other), date range restriction, HIV/AIDS/mental health/substance abuse disclosure language, revocation rights, 6-month default expiration, and references CFR 164.524. Single-page form, no fillable fields, text-based PDF.
- **File size:** 370,032 bytes

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.essehealth.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/Physician-Office-Medical-Records-and-Message-Authorizations-%E2%80%93-Allows-Us-to-Leave-Messages-and-Speak-with-a-Designated-Person-About-Medical-and-Financial-Information.pdf
- **Document type:** Communication/message authorization form (not an ROI form)
- **Description:** Authorizes Esse Health to leave messages and communicate health/billing information with designated persons. Lists contact numbers and designated individuals who may discuss diagnosis/treatment and billing info. Includes revocation section. This is a communication authorization, not a records release form.
- **File size:** ~1 page
