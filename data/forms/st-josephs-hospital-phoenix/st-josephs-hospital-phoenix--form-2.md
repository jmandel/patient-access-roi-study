## 7. NOTABLE OBSERVATIONS

### üö© RED FLAGS

- **"Acceptance" language**: The form states the hospital will "contact you with either denial or acceptance of the request." Under HIPAA ¬ß 164.524, patient access requests must be fulfilled within 30 days (with one 30-day extension); the default is fulfillment, not a discretionary "acceptance." Framing the response as acceptance-or-denial suggests a gatekeeping posture inconsistent with the right of access.

- **Employee verification line**: Page 4 includes fields for "ID Presented" and "Name of hospital employee verifying signatory information" with "Title and Department." While identity verification is reasonable, the structured employee-verification block implies in-person presentation may be expected, which could deter patients who submit remotely via email or fax.
