## Search Strategies

1. **Web search for "Cordova Community Medical Center authorization release health information form PDF"** — Immediately found a direct link to the PDF on their website at `https://www.cdvcmc.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/Release-of-Information-form.pdf`.
2. **Web search for site:cdvcmc.com medical records** — Confirmed the form is linked from the Medical Records page at `https://www.cdvcmc.com/for-patients/medical-records/`.
3. **Browsed the Medical Records page** — The page clearly explains patient rights to receive health information and links directly to the same ROI form PDF. Only one form is offered for medical records release.
4. **Checked Patient Forms page** — Contains registration, consent, minor consent, financial assistance, and payment plan forms, but no additional ROI-related forms.
