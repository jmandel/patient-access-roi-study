# Memorial Healthcare System — ROI Form Retrieval Notes

## Organization
- **Name:** Memorial Healthcare System
- **Website:** https://www.mhs.net
- **Location:** Hollywood, FL
- **Type:** Community hospital / Regional system, Government-owned

## Search Strategy

1. **Web search** for `Memorial Healthcare System Hollywood FL "authorization" "release" "health information" form PDF` — immediately found direct PDF link and the medical records page.
2. **Medical Records page** at https://www.mhs.net/patients-and-visitors/medical-records — confirmed the form and found additional language versions (Spanish, Creole) plus an Amendment Request form.

## Retrieval Difficulty: Easy

The form was straightforward to find. The medical records page is linked from the main "Patients & Visitors" navigation. The PDF is directly downloadable without any login, CAPTCHA, or bot blocking.

## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.mhs.net/-/media/mhs/files/patients-and-visitors/medical-record-request.pdf
- **Document type:** Authorization for Release of Confidential Medical Records (English)
- **Pages:** 2
- **Description:** The primary ROI form. Includes facility selection checkboxes (Memorial Regional Hospital, Joe DiMaggio Children's Hospital, Memorial Hospital West, Miramar, Pembroke, etc.), record type selection, delivery method options (Pick-up, Mail, e-Delivery, CD, MyChart), and standard HIPAA authorization language. Has a text layer. Mentions HIV/AIDS, mental health, substance abuse, and genetic testing information. Authorization expires 6 months from signature.

### form-2.pdf

- **Pages:** 2
- **Description:** Spanish translation of the same ROI form (form-1.pdf). Same structure and content.

### form-4.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.mhs.net/-/media/mhs/files/patients-and-visitors/request-for-amendment-form.pdf
- **Document type:** Request for Amendment of Protected Health Information
- **Pages:** 1 (or more)
- **Description:** Not an ROI form — this is a separate form for patients to request amendments to their medical records under HIPAA. Downloaded because it was on the same Medical Records page and is related to patient health information rights.

## Additional Notes
- The medical records page notes that records will be mailed or emailed; records will not be faxed.
- There may be copy charges as authorized by Florida law, but no charge if records are sent directly to a treating physician for continuation of care.
- Processing time is stated as within two business days.
- The form offers electronic delivery options (e-Delivery, MyChart), which is notable for patient-centeredness.
- Forms are available in 3 languages (English, Spanish, Haitian Creole), reflecting the South Florida patient population.
