## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://www.lexhealth.com/docs/default-source/privacy---legal/medical-records-release-authorization-form.pdf?sfvrsn=1377b0b6_6
- **Document**: Authorization for Release of Protected Health Information (English)
- **Description**: Two-page form covering both Medical Records Release (copies of PHI) and Verbal Communication Release. Includes checkboxes for specific record types (ED notes, lab tests, H&P, consultations, radiology, etc.), delivery method options (Portal, Mail, Pick-up, Fax), and a signature/authorization section. Has a text layer (not a scan). Not fillable (no interactive form fields).

### form-3.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://www.lexhealth.com/docs/default-source/privacy---legal/reproductive-health-attestation-form.pdf?sfvrsn=a247f8f2_5
- **Document**: Model Attestation for Requested Use or Disclosure of PHI Potentially Related to Reproductive Health Care
- **Description**: This is not an ROI form per se, but a supplemental attestation form required when requesting PHI related to reproductive health care. It implements HIPAA requirements prohibiting use/disclosure of PHI for investigations or liability related to lawful reproductive health care. It's a companion document to the main release form.
- **File size**: 256,103 bytes
