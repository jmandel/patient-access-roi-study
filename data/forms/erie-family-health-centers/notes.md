3. **Web search for site:eriefamilyhealth.org + authorization release medical records** — Confirmed the URL and also found the medical records request page at `https://www.eriefamilyhealth.org/recordsrequest/`
4. **Fetched the medical records request page** — Found links to both English and Spanish versions of the ROI form, plus instructions for submission.

## Downloads

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.eriefamilyhealth.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/ROI-English.pdf
- **File size:** 2,596,022 bytes (~2.5 MB)
- **Document type:** Authorization to Release Protected Health Information (English)
- **Description:** Erie-specific ROI form. Includes fields for patient name, DOB, PCP, release/receive direction, purpose checkboxes (personal use, disability, transfer/continuing care, legal counsel, insurance/benefits, upcoming appointment, other), format options (paper/electronic CD-ROM), delivery method (encrypted email, pickup, fax, US postal mail), health information type checkboxes (office visit notes, immunization records, school physical forms, lab reports, billing records, complete chart, prenatal records, radiology/imaging, other), date range selection, special permission checkboxes for mental/behavioral health, alcohol/substance use, HIV/AIDS. Has text layer. Requires copy of ID. Auto-expires in 6 months.
