# UCHealth University of Colorado Hospital — ROI Form Retrieval Notes

## Search Strategies

1. **Web search** for `UCHealth University of Colorado Hospital "authorization" "release" "health information" form PDF` — returned direct PDF URLs from uchealth.org.
2. **Web search** for `UCHealth "medical records" "release form" request records PDF` — returned additional PDF URLs including one from uchealth.com (different org).
3. **Navigated** the UCHealth medical records page at `https://www.uchealth.org/access-my-health-connection/medical-records-uchealth/` — found the current official form linked directly, plus an online request portal (MRO Express).

The medical records page was easy to find and clearly organized with download links, online request option, and contact info for each UCHealth location.

## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf

- **File size**: 483,507 bytes (1 page)
- **Description**: Older UCHealth "Authorization to Disclose Protected Health Information" form. Lists University of Colorado Hospital, Poudre Valley Hospital, Medical Center of the Rockies, Memorial Hospital, and Colorado Health Medical Group. Includes checkboxes for record types, purpose of request, and signature requirements. Has text layer. Appears to be a 2017-era version.

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://www.uchealth.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/RSO-Patient-Info-Release.pdf
- **File size**: 234,916 bytes
- **Description**: UCHealth "Authorization to Release and/or Obtain Patient Information" form. Specifically references University of Colorado Hospital. Includes fields for obtaining from and releasing to facilities, record type checkboxes, and mentions that once disclosed, information may no longer be protected by UCH. Appears to be a 2018-era form variant, possibly for inter-facility transfers.

### form-3.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://www.uchealth.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/UCH-ROI-01-Release-of-Information-040621-Final1.pdf
- **File size**: 155,083 bytes (2 pages)
- **Description**: **Wrong organization.** This is from UC Health Cincinnati (University of Cincinnati Medical Center), NOT UCHealth Colorado. The uchealth.com domain belongs to the Cincinnati system, while uchealth.org is the Colorado system. The form references Daniel Drake Center, University of Cincinnati Medical Center, and West Chester Hospital. Downloaded because the web search returned this URL; kept per study protocol.

### form-4.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://uchealth-wp-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/21090243/HIM19000.1025-Authorization-to-Disclose-PHI.pdf
- **File size**: 142,641 bytes (1 page)
- **Description**: **Current official UCHealth Colorado** "Authorization to Disclose Protected Health Information" form. Lists all UCHealth facilities including University of Colorado Hospital, Broomfield, Greeley, Highlands Ranch, Longs Peak, Medical Center of the Rockies, Memorial, Poudre Valley, Yampa Valley, Parkview, Grandview, and Pikes Peak Regional. Includes electronic delivery options (email, My Health Connection portal, PowerShare for radiology images). Has fillable checkboxes and text fields. This is the most current version, linked from the official medical records request page.

## Difficulties

- **Domain confusion**: uchealth.org (Colorado) vs uchealth.com (Cincinnati) are different health systems. The .com URL returned a form from the wrong organization.
- **No other significant difficulties**: Forms were easy to find via both web search and direct website navigation. The medical records page is clearly organized with direct PDF download links.

## Summary

UCHealth makes their ROI form readily available online. The current form (form-4.pdf) is linked directly from their medical records page and includes modern features like electronic delivery options and a comprehensive facility list. They also offer an online request portal through MRO Express. The form was found within 1-2 clicks from the main website navigation.
