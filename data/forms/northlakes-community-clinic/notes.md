# NorthLakes Community Clinic — ROI Form Retrieval Notes

## Search Strategies
- Web search for `NorthLakes Community Clinic "medical records" "release form" filetype:pdf` — immediately returned two PDF URLs hosted on nlccwi.org.
- Web search for `site:nlccwi.org authorization release medical records` — confirmed the same two forms and identified the medical records page at https://nlccwi.org/for-our-patients/medical-records/.

## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://nlccwi.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/ExternalROI-122023.pdf
- **File size**: 197,179 bytes
- **Description**: "Release of Information" form (dated 12/2023). This is the primary external ROI form. It includes sections for personal information, release purpose, release FROM/TO fields, type of information to release (health records, mental health, substance use disorder, HIV/STD), date ranges, and signature/authorization. It mentions faxing to 715-318-5644 and has an option for encrypted email delivery. Has a text layer (not a scan).

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL**: https://nlccwi.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/Health-Information-Release-Form-Fillable-422.pdf
- **File size**: 153,560 bytes
- **Description**: "Health Information Release" form (fillable, dated 4/2022). An older/alternative ROI form with similar content — patient info, release to/from, purpose checkboxes, record type selection (entire medical file, dental, mental health, substance use disorder, etc.), date ranges, and consent language referencing 42 CFR Part 2 and HIPAA 45 CFR. Described as fillable in the filename. Has a text layer.
