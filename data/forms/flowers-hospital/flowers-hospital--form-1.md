## 6. COMPLIANCE

**Authorization expiration:** *"This authorization will remain in effect for one (1) year or until I revoke it in writing."* A one-year expiration is standard and reasonable.

**Revocation rights:** Clearly stated: *"I may cancel this consent at any time in writing to Wiregrass Surgical Associates, P.C."* with an acknowledgment that prior disclosures remain valid.

**Liability waiver:** The form includes: *"I hereby release Wiregrass Surgical Associates, P.C. and its employees from any and all liability that may arise from the release of information as I have directed."* This is a broad liability release bundled into the authorization, which could discourage patients from filing complaints about improper disclosure.

**Fee structure:** The disclosed per-page fees ($1.00/page for first 25, $0.50/page thereafter, plus $5.00 search fee) follow Alabama Code ¬ß 12-21-6.1 for third-party requests. However, if a patient is requesting their own records under HIPAA's Right of Access, the fee must be limited to a reasonable, cost-based fee ‚Äî which may be lower than this statutory schedule. **Treatment conditionality:** The form correctly states: *"I further understand that my ability to obtain treatment will not depend in any way on whether or not I sign this authorization."*
