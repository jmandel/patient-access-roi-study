## 6. COMPLIANCE

- **Wrong-jurisdiction form**: This BRMC form is branded for a Pennsylvania facility. Cuba Memorial Hospital is in New York. Pennsylvania and New York have different health privacy laws ‚Äî for example, different rules around mental health records, minor consent, and substance abuse treatment records. Using a PA-branded form for a NY facility could create confusion about which state's privacy protections apply.
- **Witness line**: The form includes "Witness" and "Witness/Interpreter (if needed)" signature lines with date/time fields. HIPAA does not require a witness for authorization forms. The presence of these lines ‚Äî particularly without a note that witnessing is optional ‚Äî may deter patients from submitting without a witness present.
- **Photo ID for pickup**: The bolded requirement "**Photo ID is required for records to be picked up**" implies in-person pickup is the expected method. HIPAA requires covered entities to provide records in the form and format requested by the patient when readily producible.
- **Specially protected information**: Section 7 uses separate consent checkboxes for HIV/AIDS, psychiatric/mental health, and substance abuse ‚Äî consistent with 42 CFR Part 2 requirements. These are check-to-include rather than check-to-exclude, meaning patients must affirmatively authorize inclusion of these categories.
- **Staff Use Only**: Includes "Unable to release ____ due to ____" for documenting partial denials, though no appeal or complaint process is mentioned.
