# Owensboro Health — ROI Form Retrieval Notes

## Search Strategy
- Web search for `Owensboro Health "authorization" "release" "health information" form PDF` immediately returned direct links to two PDFs on owensborohealth.org.
- A second search for `Owensboro Health medical records release form request` confirmed the same URLs and also surfaced their medical records page at `/locations/owensboro-health-regional-hospital-home/about-your-stay/medical-records`.

## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.owensborohealth.org/sites/default/files/inline/b7/health-disclosure-form.pdf
- **File size:** 187,873 bytes
- **Description:** "Authorization for Use and Disclosure of Health Information" — the primary ROI form. Includes patient info fields, requested format options (Paper, MyChart, EHI Export, Email, Fax, CD, USB), delivery method options, reason/purpose checkboxes, facility selection (Regional Hospital, Muhlenberg Community Hospital, Twin Lakes Medical Center, Medical Group), recipient info, date range, record type checkboxes, special protected records section, and signature/date fields.
- **Notable features:** Offers EHI Export option with explanation that it covers all ePHI across the organization and Community Connect Partners. Mentions 120-day expiration. Has text layer (not a scan).

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.owensborohealth.org/sites/default/files/inline/1a/health-disclosure-form-instructions.pdf
- **File size:** 205,930 bytes
- **Description:** "Authorization for the Use and Disclosure of Health Information (INSTRUCTIONS)" — a companion document explaining how to complete the authorization form. Covers each section of the form, explains EHI export, delivery methods, special protected records, expiration, patient rights, and where to send completed requests (with addresses/fax/email for OHRH and OHMCH).

## Difficulties
- None. Both forms were directly accessible via simple web search and curl download without any bot blocking or login walls.
