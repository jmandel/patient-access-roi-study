# Merit Health Central - ROI Form Retrieval Notes

## Organization
- **Name:** Merit Health Central
- **Location:** Jackson, MS
- **Website:** https://www.merithealthcentral.com
- **Type:** Community hospital, for-profit, part of Community Health Systems (CHS)/Merit Health system

## Search Strategy
1. **Web search** for "Merit Health Central Jackson MS authorization release health information medical records form PDF" — immediately found the dedicated medical records request page.
2. **Navigated** to https://www.merithealthcentral.com/request-medical-records — found direct PDF download links for English and Spanish versions of the authorization form.

## What Worked
The web search immediately pointed to the correct page. The form was easy to find — linked from the "Request Medical Records" page under Patients & Visitors, with clear "English" and "Spanish" download links.

## Downloaded Forms

### form-1.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.merithealthcentral.com/Uploads/Public/Documents/all-new-documents/HIM-1406.pdf
- **File size:** 102,982 bytes
- **Document type:** Authorization for Release of Health Information (English version)
- **Initial impression:** Well-structured ROI form with text layer (fillable). Includes patient information fields, specific record type checkboxes (progress notes, ER records, discharge summary, H&P, labs, imaging, etc.), sensitive information disclosure options (alcohol/drug abuse, HIV, genetic testing, psychiatric), delivery method choices (paper, email, removable media, mail, pick up in person), and recipient designation (self, personal representative, third party). Notably offers electronic delivery options including email and removable media with password protection options.

### form-2.pdf
- **Source URL:** https://www.merithealthcentral.com/Uploads/Public/Documents/all-new-documents/HIM-1406S.pdf
- **File size:** 284,813 bytes
- **Document type:** Authorization for Release of Health Information (Spanish version)
- **Initial impression:** Spanish translation of the same ROI form (HIM-1406S). Contains identical structure and fields as the English version. Larger file size may indicate different font embedding for Spanish characters.

## Difficulties
- None. The forms were prominently linked on the medical records request page and downloaded without any issues.

## Additional Observations
- The website also offers an online request process via Swellbox (https://www.swellbox.com/merit-health-jackson-wizard.html) as an alternative to the downloadable PDF form.
- The page distinguishes between patient/patient representative requests and provider requests.
- FAQ section on the page addresses authorization expiration, charges, and who can request records.
