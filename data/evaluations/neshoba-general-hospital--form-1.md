## 6. COMPLIANCE

Because this is not an ROI form, standard compliance analysis (barriers to patient access, fee disclosure, expiration terms, consent bundling) does not apply. However, the absence of any online ROI form is itself a compliance-adjacent concern:

- **HIPAA's Right of Access** (45 CFR ¬ß 164.524) requires covered entities to act on patient requests for their records. While HIPAA does not explicitly require publishing an ROI form online, making no form available digitally ‚Äî and having no "Medical Records" page on the website ‚Äî creates a practical barrier. Patients must call or visit in person just to learn how to initiate a records request.
- The hospital's website lacks even a HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices PDF, which covered entities are required to provide and which typically explains how patients can request their records.
