## 7. NOTABLE OBSERVATIONS

### üö© RED FLAGS

- **Physician approval gate for patient access**: The "HOSPITAL USE ONLY" section includes a physician approval/denial workflow ("APPROVED by Physician Name" / "DENIED ‚Äì REASON FOR DENIAL"). Under HIPAA ¬ß 164.524, a covered entity may deny access only in narrow circumstances (e.g., psychotherapy notes, information compiled for legal proceedings). A blanket physician-approval gate on patient access requests could constitute an unreasonable barrier if used broadly. The form states: "Dependent on State Regulations, authorization from the physician who attended the patient during their stay may be required."

- **Fee warning without amounts or HIPAA limits**: The form warns "there may be a fee" and "(A fee may be charged)" but provides no information about fee amounts, HIPAA's cost-based fee limitation for patient access requests, or any distinction between patient-access fees and third-party release fees. Under HIPAA, fees for patient access are limited to reasonable, cost-based amounts.
