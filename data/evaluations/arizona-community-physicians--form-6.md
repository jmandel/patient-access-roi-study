## 5. PATIENT-CENTEREDNESS

**Purpose and audience**: This form serves a specific and practical need ‚Äî allowing Spanish-speaking parents/guardians to designate who may access their child's medical information. In Tucson, AZ, where approximately 42% of the population is Hispanic, providing this form in Spanish is meaningful.

**Scope of coverage**: The Spanish translation covers only the minor access-designation form, not the primary ROI form (form-1), adult access designation (form-3), imaging release (form-4), or HIPAA amendment request (form-7). A Spanish-speaking parent who needs to request a full copy of their child's medical records would need to use the English-only form-1, which is a significant gap.

## 6. COMPLIANCE

**HIPAA considerations**: This form is a standing consent/access designation, not a HIPAA authorization for disclosure to a third party. It governs internal sharing practices (who can call about appointments, receive test results, etc.) and is more closely related to facility policies than to 45 CFR ¬ß 164.508 authorization requirements.

**No expiration date**: Unlike form-1's explicit one-year expiration, this form has no stated expiration. The parent can revoke via written notice, but absent revocation, the permissions appear to remain in effect indefinitely. The English version (form-5) is identical in this regard. While not necessarily a compliance issue, indefinite standing authorizations could become stale if family circumstances change.

**No notarization or witness requirements**: Only the parent/guardian signature and date are required ‚Äî no notary, witness, or in-person submission is demanded.

**16+ minor seen alone**: The form includes initialing options for allowing minors 16+ to be seen without an adult and to receive minor procedures or immunizations unaccompanied. The bottom note appropriately clarifies that this general consent "no sustituye el consentimiento informado por escrito por separado" (does not substitute for separate written informed consent) for procedures ‚Äî a meaningful liability and compliance protection.
